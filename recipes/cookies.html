Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a beloved baked treat made with a dough that typically includes butter, sugar, flour, and eggs, mixed with generous amounts of chocolate chips. Known for its sweet, buttery flavor and delightful texture, chocolate chip cookies can range from soft and chewy to crispy, depending on the recipe. The melty chocolate chunks and golden-brown edges make these cookies a favorite comfort food and a staple in homes and bakeries worldwide. Enjoyed with a glass of milk, they offer a perfect blend of sweetness and indulgence in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Butter

  • Flour
