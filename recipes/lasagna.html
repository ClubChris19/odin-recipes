Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish composed of layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel (or ricotta), and melted cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. Each layer combines to create a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, with the pasta absorbing the savory sauces.

Traditionally baked in an oven until the top is golden and bubbling, lasagna is a comforting and hearty meal enjoyed worldwide. Variations abound, from the traditional meat-based Bolognese sauce to vegetarian versions with spinach and ricotta or a medley of roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna as per the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Allow the lasagna to rest before serving.
